Florian Mod (straight cut)

In this demo the inner corners have been cut off to resemble what modern physical cubes meant for speedcubing actually look like. The amount can be adjusted, enter a number in the box and press Enter (must be >= 1, can be decimal, less is more). The corners that are modded are configurable as can be seen on the second cube (see function drawFlorian in A3_florian.js for implementation details).
