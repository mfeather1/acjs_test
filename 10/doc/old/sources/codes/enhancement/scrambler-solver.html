Scrambler and Solver

How many random moves can you unscramble? Press the Scramble button to scramble the cube by the number of moves shown, then try to solve it. To see the solution, press Rewind button (leftmost one) and then press Play button (right arrow) or Single-Step button next to the Play button. For a different number of scrambling moves change the number in the box and press the Enter key (or the Scramble button).
